## **Design Process**  

1. **Initial Consultation**  
   Discuss design needs, style preferences, and project goals.  
2. **On-site Measurement**  
   Inspect structure, dimensions, lighting, and utilities using professional tools.  
3. **Design Proposal**  
   - Present ≥2 layout plans with 3D models for key spaces.  
   - Refine designs through iterative client feedback[7](@ref)[8](@ref).  

---

## **Construction Process (e.g., ADU)**  

1. Site excavation and leveling.  
2. Structural reinforcement (steel bars, sewers).  
3. Concrete pouring.  
4. Wood framing and roof installation.  
5. Exterior walls with waterproofing/fireproofing.  
6. Plumbing, electrical, and gas line installation.  
7. Wall/ceiling insulation.  
8. Drywall finishing for interior work[6](@ref).
